About

I’m a postdoc in mathematics at the University of Hamburg in the group of Christoph Schweigert. Previously, I was a PhD student at TU Dresden under the supervision of Ulrich Krähmer.

Broadly, my work in mathematics lives under the umbrella of monoidal category theory and categorical reconstruction theory.
Not-quite-so-incidentally, the latter is also the name of my dissertation, which you can access here. I’ve also written about some typographical aspects of it.
In particular, I like thinking about Hopf monads; graphical calculi; rigid, linearly distributive, Grothendieck–Verdier, and duoidal categories; as well as operads. If you’re interested in my publications, you can check out my name on the arXiv. I’ve also given some talks, with a few slides available here.
